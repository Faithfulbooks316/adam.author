About the Author

A Bold Voice at the
Edge of Hidden Truth

Adam Joseph Hanrahan is a bold voice in contemporary literature, fearlessly exploring the intersections of ancient wisdom, modern conspiracy, spiritual warfare, and cutting-edge technology.

Through FaithfulBooks Publishing, his work spans multiple genres — from supernatural thrillers and speculative fiction to hard-hitting non-fiction exposés. Each book is crafted not only to entertain, but to awaken, challenge, and equip readers to confront the unseen systems shaping modern life.

Self-taught through seven years of trial and error, Hanrahan developed a reliable method to go from conception to publishing within three months — and now mentors other first-time authors while continuing to publish his own expanding catalog.
